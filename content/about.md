# About AI for Everyone

## Our Mission

We believe that understanding AI shouldn't require a computer science degree. Our mission is to make AI accessible, practical, and approachable for everyone.

## Who We Are

AI for Everyone is dedicated to providing high-quality, approachable education about artificial intelligence for:

- **Professionals** looking to enhance their productivity
- **Parents** navigating AI with their children
- **Business owners** wanting to leverage AI for growth
- **Anyone curious** about this transformative technology

## Our Approach

::alert{color="green"}
**Learning by Doing**
Our courses focus on practical applications you can implement immediately, not abstract theory.
::

::alert{color="blue"}
**No Prerequisites Required**
We assume no technical background. If you can use a computer, you can learn AI with us.
::

::alert{color="purple"}
**Real-World Examples**
Every lesson includes concrete examples from everyday work and life.
::
