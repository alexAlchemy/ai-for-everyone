# Introduction to AI in the Workplace

Welcome to your AI journey! In this lesson, we'll explore what artificial intelligence really means and how it's already transforming the way we work.

## What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence refers to computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. This includes:

- Understanding and generating text
- Recognizing patterns in data
- Making predictions and recommendations
- Automating repetitive tasks

## AI in Your Daily Work

You're probably already using AI more than you realize:

::alert{color="blue"}
**Examples of AI at Work:**
- Email spam filters
- Grammar and writing suggestions
- Virtual meeting transcriptions
- Smart calendar scheduling
::

## Why AI Matters Now

The recent advances in AI technology have made these tools more accessible and powerful than ever before. Understanding how to work alongside AI is becoming as essential as knowing how to use email or spreadsheets.

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course, you'll be able to:
- Identify tasks where AI can add value
- Choose the right AI tools for your needs
- Use AI effectively and ethically
- Integrate AI into your daily workflow
