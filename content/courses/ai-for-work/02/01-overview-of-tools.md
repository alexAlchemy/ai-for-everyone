# Overview of AI Tools

The AI tool landscape is rapidly evolving. This lesson will help you navigate the options and choose tools that fit your needs.

## Categories of AI Tools

### 1. General-Purpose AI Assistants
Tools that can help with a wide range of tasks:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Google Gemini
- Microsoft Copilot

### 2. Specialized Writing Tools
- Grammarly (grammar and tone)
- Jasper (marketing copy)
- Copy.ai (content generation)

### 3. Research and Knowledge Tools
- Perplexity (AI search)
- Notion AI (note-taking and organization)
- Research assistants

### 4. Productivity Integrations
- AI features in Microsoft 365
- Google Workspace AI
- Slack AI

## Choosing the Right Tool

Consider these factors:

1. **Your Primary Use Case**: What will you use it for most?
2. **Integration Needs**: Does it work with your existing tools?
3. **Privacy Requirements**: Does your work have data restrictions?
4. **Cost**: Free tier sufficient or need enterprise features?
5. **Learning Curve**: How quickly do you need results?

## Getting Started Recommendations

::alert{color="blue"}
**Beginner's Stack:**
Start with one general-purpose AI assistant (ChatGPT or Claude) to learn the basics. Add specialized tools as you identify specific needs.
::
