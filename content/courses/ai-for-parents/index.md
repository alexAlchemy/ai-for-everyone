Navigate the world of AI with your children. This course helps you understand AI technology, guide your kids safely, and use AI tools to enhance family life.

## Course Modules

Build your foundation in AI and learn how it affects your children's world.

### [Module 02: AI Safety and Digital Citizenship](/courses/ai-for-parents/02)
Learn how to keep your kids safe while using AI tools and teach responsible AI use.
