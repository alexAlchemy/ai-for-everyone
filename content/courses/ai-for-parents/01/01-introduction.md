# Introduction to AI for Families

AI is increasingly part of children's livesâ€”from homework help to entertainment. As a parent, understanding this technology helps you guide your kids effectively.

## Why This Matters

Your children are growing up in an AI-powered world:
- Schools are introducing AI tools
- Kids encounter AI in apps and games
- AI affects their future career prospects
- Digital literacy now includes AI literacy

## What You'll Learn

This course will help you:
- Understand what AI is in kid-friendly terms
- Recognize when and how your kids use AI
- Guide healthy and productive AI use
- Use AI tools to support your family
- Teach responsible AI citizenship

## AI Is Already Here

::alert{color="blue"}
**AI Your Kids Already Use:**
- Voice assistants (Siri, Alexa)
- Video recommendations (YouTube, Netflix)
- Homework helpers
- Educational apps
- Chat and social media filters
::

## Your Role as a Parent

You don't need to be a tech expert! Your role is to:
- Stay informed about what your kids are using
- Set boundaries and guidelines
- Encourage critical thinking
- Model responsible technology use

## Getting Comfortable

If AI feels overwhelming, that's normal. This course breaks everything down into practical, actionable steps.
